Col des Mosses (el. 1445 m) is a high mountain pass in the Swiss Alps at Les Mosses in the canton of Vaud, linking Aigle to Château-d'Œx.

The Alpine Pass Route hiking trail passes through Col des Mosses on the way to Montreux.
