Archidium is a genus of about 35 species of moss; it is the only genus in the family Archidiaceae and order Archidiales.

Classification[edit]

The genus Archidium includes 35 species (this list may be incomplete):

  • Archidium alternifolium
  • Archidium donnellii
  • Archidium elatum, Dixon & Sainsbury
  • Archidium hallii
  • Archidium minus
  • Archidium ohioense
  • Archidium stellatum
  • Archidium tenerrimum
